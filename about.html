About us

Sprout & Grow are founded and located in Raleigh, North carolina.
We seeked to cultivate high-quality organic plants, carefully and meticulously nurtured with sustainable gardening practices.
Our values: Trust, Innovation, Integrity, Transparency, and enviromental sustainability

History

Sprout & Grow was founded in raleigh, by Katie & Calvin Rosas.
Mr. and Mrs. Rosas dreamed of building a place where enviromental sustainability and share their knowledge on organic gardening practices to the community.



Mission

Our mission at Sprout & Grow is to be NC's premier destination for high-quality organic plants.
We offer a diverse selection of meticulously nurtured plants, expert guidance, and educational resources to empower individuals in cultivating thriving organic gardens.
Our nursery is committed to transparent and ethical practices, fostering sustainability and environmental stewardship within the community.
